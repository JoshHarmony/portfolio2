Relay: Concept Project

Timeframe: 2-week sprint

Relay came about through the exploration of a problem space, in this case, the need for artists to find other artists to collaborate with.
This solo project aimed to create a platform in which creatives from different disciplines could come together and create things which are better than the sum of its parts.

The project relied heavily on usability testing as I tried to find the best and most intuitive way to to display all content which included a redesign of the app's nav bar.
