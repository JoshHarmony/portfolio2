My Process

I see my working tools as an extension of my workplace. For this reason I believe that organization and structure are essential to being creative and productive. When creating prototypes and mockups I make sure to organize the content in order to optimize the workflow and make revisions a breeze.

DISCOVER

Research, gather information and discover insights about the task at hand. This first stage serves as the stepping stone for the whole project and it helps identify the problem that needs to be solved. I aim to achieve this by doing competitive analysis, task analysis, story boards, developing personas and user flow.

DEFINE

Document, compile and focus.Organizing the information gathered during the Discover stage is essential to moving forward with the project. Defining which discoveries are most important lets me focus on a few possible solutions and helps me answer questions such as: What's our main challenge? What's possible and attainable? and What should we attempt to solve first?

DEVELOP

Design, test and iterate. After doing the research and focusing on select areas I strive to find solutions to the problems specified earlier. Prototyping and user testing are the main focus here. Iteration is key since it makes clear what are the flaws in the design and which things need to be ironed out.

DELIVER

Solutions, finalization, launch.The final stage in the process consists of finalizing the product so it's ready to launch.

This is an iterative process and it may be repeated several times, even after launch. This helps to ensure great quality and outstanding user experience, which in turns translate into business objectives and increased revenue.
